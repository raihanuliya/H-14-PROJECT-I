About the Project

Built on creativity, collaboration, and unparelled service. The H-14 Interior Design experience is focused on crafting the ideal environment for each client. With over 10 years of combined design practice their highly skilled and accredited interior designers are equipped to undertake projects of any sizes including both Commercial and Residential.

"The essence of interior design will always be about people and how they live. It is about the realities of what makes for an attractive, civilized, meaningful environment, not about fashion or what's in or what's out. This is not an easy job." -Albert Hadley
